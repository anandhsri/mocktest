# Mathematics Chapters Used in the Test

This document lists all the chapters from CBSE Class 10 Mathematics syllabus that are covered in the mock test questions.

## Complete List of Chapters (14 chapters)

1. **Real Numbers**
2. **Polynomials**
3. **Pair of Linear Equations in Two Variables**
4. **Quadratic Equations**
5. **Arithmetic Progressions**
6. **Introduction to Trigonometry**
7. **Some Applications of Trigonometry**
8. **Coordinate Geometry**
9. **Triangles**
10. **Circles**
11. **Surface Areas and Volumes**
12. **Areas Related to Circles**
13. **Statistics**
14. **Probability**

## Chapter Distribution

The 50 questions are distributed across these 14 chapters, with questions from the last 3 years (2021-2023) of CBSE question papers. All questions are of **tough difficulty level**.

## CBSE Class 10 Mathematics Syllabus Coverage

These chapters cover the complete CBSE Class 10 Mathematics syllabus:
- **Number Systems**: Real Numbers
- **Algebra**: Polynomials, Pair of Linear Equations, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic Progressions
- **Trigonometry**: Introduction to Trigonometry, Some Applications of Trigonometry
- **Geometry**: Coordinate Geometry, Triangles, Circles
- **Mensuration**: Surface Areas and Volumes, Areas Related to Circles
- **Statistics and Probability**: Statistics, Probability
